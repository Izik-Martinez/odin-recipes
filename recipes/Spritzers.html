Spritzers

Description

Known in German as "Schorle" (fruit spritzers), this juice and sparkling water mixture is very popular in Germany. The sparkling water used is high in essential minerals which are important for hydrating during the hot summer months.


Ingredients

  • 1 part juice or wine
  • 1 part sparkling mineral water

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients together.
  2. Adjust the ratio to your preference - some like 1:1, some 1:2, and some 1:3!
